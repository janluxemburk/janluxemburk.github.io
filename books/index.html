Most of the important things I’ve learned have come from books, articles, and technical papers.

Technical Books

Introduction to Modern Cryptography by Jonathan Katz and‎ Yehuda Lindell.

Security Engineering by Ross J. Anderson. March 2018. Available here.

High Performance Browser Networking by Ilya Grigorik. February 2018. Available at hpbn.co.

Cryptography Engineering by Niels Ferguson, Bruce Schneier and Tadayoshi Kohno. October 2017. My first book on cryptography and security. This is a good review of the book.

The Mythical Man Month by Frederick P. Brooks. August 2017.

An Introduction to Functional Programming Through Lambda Calculus by Greg Michaelson. February 2017. The primary source for my Bachelor’s thesis.

Exploring ES6 by Axel Rauschmayer. October 2016. Author’s blog 2ality.com is one of the best JavaScript resourses.

JavaScript: The Good Parts by Douglas Crockford. May 2016.

Javascript Allongé by Reginald Braithwaite. May 2016. My first book on functional programming and JavaScript.
